Class: MenuItems

Attributes:
servingSize (integer)
vegetarian (boolean)
addOns (array)
initialPrice (float)

Methods:
applyCoupon (if someone has a coupon for a specific menu item, the price attribute will change)
addMeat (this will change the vegetarian attribute to false, and also effect the array attribute, as well as the price)
orderHalfSize (This will divide the serving size in half, and also change the price attribute)
substituteItems (this will effect the addOns array, as well as the vegetarian attribute depending if you get/substitute meat, and also may)
