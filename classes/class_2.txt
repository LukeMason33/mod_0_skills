Class: BarDrinks
Attributes:
alcoholContent (float)
size (integer)
isBeerOnTap (boolean)
colorOfDrink (string)

Methods:
makeItADouble (will change the size attribute, as well as the alcoholContent attribute)
finishKeg (this will change the isBeerOnTap attribute)
addJuice (this will change the color attribute)
trySample (this will reduce the size of the drink)
